Add calendar events with ease using Slate Scheduler!

Slate does away with clunky date pickers and scroll wheels to give you a quick and seamless way to add calendar events to your favorite calendar services like Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, and more!

By using autocomplete, Slate can help you add your common events more efficiently than any other calendar app. Happy scheduling!

** Slate does not share your personal data. It is stored completely locally on your device. **
